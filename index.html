About me

Hello, I'm Mert, an IT Specialist based in Istanbul, Turkey. I focus on system security and networking, aiming to develop secure, efficient, and reliable systems while continuously expanding my expertise in this field.

My role covers a wide range of responsibilities in both system and network management. On the systems side, I work with Dell, HP, and IBM servers, setting up iDRAC and iLO configurations, creating RAID sets, and virtualizing servers using VMware or Proxmox. Within these virtual environments, I install and configure essential Windows and Linux servers to ensure seamless operations.

In networking, I manage everything from planning and cabling to consulting on products, and I program switches and routers to maintain a robust network infrastructure. When it comes to security, I handle the installation and configuration of critical software like firewalls, NAC, and SIEM, ensuring all systems are secure and compliant.

My goal is to stay at the forefront of IT advancements, continually refining my skills and embracing new technologies to provide the most effective solutions. I’m committed to delivering expertise and reliability in everything I do, always driven to take my work to the next level.
